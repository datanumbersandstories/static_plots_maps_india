Back in 2014, the Government of India, in an attempt to promote renewable energy usage, put forth an ambition for India, in developing a capacity to produce 100 Giga-Watts of Solar Power, by the year 2022. In accordance with this plan, the Ministry of New & Renewable Energy set aside target capacities for each state of India. Today, standing at the threshold of that deadline, we take a look at where each state stands in meeting its assigned target.
